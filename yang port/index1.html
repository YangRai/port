About Me


Something about me


You may have already guessed by now, my name is Yangbo Rai. I have been living in london since August 2009, I am currently working as a barista for Tate catering company based at Tate Britain, Millbank and honestly speaking I absolutely love my job as a barista because it gives me a chance to meet new people every day and bring smile to their faces with a lovely cup of coffee but working in the hospitality industry for over 5 years has made me realise that I need to use the experience that I have gained so far to practise the dream that I have always had: to be a web developer.

Due to my passion for technology , creativity and design, I decided to study Computer Science at the University Of Hertfordshire. Now that I have successfully completed my studies, I am looking for an opportunity that will allow me to practise my knowledge and skills that I have managed to develop over the years. In short, I am a creative and enthusiastic individual looking for challenges to develop my career as a front end developer and doing what i love doing the most.
